About OVERKILL

OVERKILL – a Starbreeze Studio is a game development company that was founded in September 2009 by a group of hardened industry veterans. As time passed loads of cool members joined up – to execute on the PAYDAY plan. At OVERKILL we go all the way – and then some! For fun!

We got lots of creative people who will soon smack talk right here on OVERKILL.

For now know this:

Our passion is to create kick-ass cooperative action games.

That, is OVERKILL.

 

MEET THE PROFESSIONALS

OVERKILL is a blend of industry veterans, freshly graduated specialists and great partners. It’s a veritable melting pot of experience and ambition. It is a new approach to development that will kill over the traditions.

The company hierarchy is flat and lean, the work process is streamlined yet creative and the turnaround time is close to instant.

We set our own deadlines, production rules and design our own pipeline – not an elite design company but a true inspired gameplay machine. It is OVERKILL at its finest.
